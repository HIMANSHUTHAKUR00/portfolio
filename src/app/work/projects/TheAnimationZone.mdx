## Overview

In this project, I assured that i provide my client a better platform to represent his art & design & a personal website of himself to showcast it to any company or any person.  The goal was to create a portfolio that my client finds it worthy to showcast his talent & skills.

## Key Features

- **About**: Personal Details & skills section.
- **Resume**: Section to provide a CV.
- **Portfolio**: Section To show the art & design .
- **Contact**: Section to provide contact details.
